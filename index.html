Our Company

is an Ethiopian based import and export company dealing with different kinds of agricultural products such as oil seeds, pulses, and grains. It was established in 2010 and we are exporting agricultural products.

The Products

The products that we are dealing with are mainly oil seeds ,grains and pulses. Under pulse we have items like Green Mung Bean, Soya Bean, Chick Pea, White Pea Bean and Pinto Bean, lupines. Under oil seed we have Sesame seed, Sunflower, Niger seed, Mustard seed,Ground nuts, castor seed. Our company was established with the aspiration of exporting food commodities from Ethiopia to other countries.
